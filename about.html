A little about me...

I am a recent biology graduate with an artistic flare and a love of challenges. My journey into coding began with college coursework in statistics programming and animation. I took the leap and invested in online coursework in front-end development and from there I was hooked. Soon I was signed up for a full stack program to learn web development and optimize my front-end skills.

In the last year, I have immersed myself in JavaScript, front-end technologies, and server side communication. I love taking on complex real world problems that exercise my coding knowledge and self teaching abilities. Whether it is creating a travel budget app or a mobile cookbook, I bring my confidence, nerdiness, and artistic wow to create user friendly applications.
